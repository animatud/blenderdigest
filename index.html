Blender & Beyond
Blender Is Looking For Help Testing Its Maturing Vulkan Backend
The upcoming Blender 4.5 build features a new Vulkan-based backend for the UI and EEVEE — and the dev team needs help testing it. Vulkan aims to replace OpenGL for better performance and future-proofing. It’s not yet production-ready, but Blender hopes to change that soon — with your help. Learn more
Chaos Adds Traffic Simulation to Anima 6
Chaos Adds Traffic Simulation to Anima 6. Anima 6 now supports traffic simulation — vehicles follow real-world driving rules, handle collisions, and interact with pedestrians automatically. Learn more
Digital Painting & Animation Software Howler 2023 Goes Free
It includes natural media brushes, basic 3D rendering (great for landscapes), frame-by-frame animation tools, and even lip-sync support. Its creator, Dan Ritchie — a former Star Trek: Voyager VFX artist — is now raising $3,000 to release the current version (Howler 2025.5) as freeware. Learn more

BLENDER MYTHBUSTER GAME 🧩
Myth or Reality?
— Cycles is always better than Eevee.
Answer: Myth

Why:

Cycles and Eevee serve different purposes.
Cycles offers photorealism — great for final renders.

Eevee is faster — ideal for previews, real-time projects, or stylized looks.
It’s not about “better,” it’s about what you need.
